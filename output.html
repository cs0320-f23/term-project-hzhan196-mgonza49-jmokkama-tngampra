Explore Programs

Discover which study abroad program best aligns with your goals and interests.

There are a host of factors that go into deciding which program is the best fit for a student. Our advisors are here to support you to identify the study abroad experience that fits your goals,interests, and needs. On-site program experience, course offerings, cultural activities, and housing options all vary based on program and program type.

Brown offers several types of programs including Distinctively Brown programs, Consortium for Advanced Studies Abroad (CASA) programs, Non-Brown Approved Programs, and Summer programs.

Below you’ll find a list of all programs that are generally approved at Brown. Due to ongoing program restrictions as a result of COVID, there are some programs listed below that are not yet reactivated. To view all approved programs for the current term, please visit our application system viaTRM. Note that you’ll need a Brown University log in to access the system.
